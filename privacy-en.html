Privacy Policy

Athlete Manager ／ Last updated: June 27, 2026

"Athlete Manager" (the "App") is an application that helps track and field athletes manage their own training and condition. This policy explains what information the App collects, how it is used, when it is shared with third parties, and your rights as a user. By using the App, you agree to this policy.

1. Information We Collect and How We Use It

The App collects and stores the following information to provide its features. All data is associated with your own account.

InformationPurpose
Email address (or Apple / Google account credentials)Account creation, authentication, and login
Name, date of birth, gender, height, weight and other physical dataProfile display, age calculation, and basis for nutrition calculations (PFC / calories)
Training records, race results, personal bests, etc.Saving and managing training and competition records
Condition data (sleep, fatigue, pain, mental scores), weight logsProviding condition-tracking features
Meal and nutrition recordsProviding nutrition-tracking features
Photos, videos, and PDFs attached to training recordsAttaching and storing files with records
Location (latitude / longitude)Used only temporarily to display local weather (not stored)
Food barcode numbersRetrieving nutrition information for food items (not stored)
Device settings (language, notification on/off, etc.)Retaining app settings (stored only on your device)

The App handles health-related information such as condition, weight, and meals, as well as personal information including date of birth. This information is used solely to provide your own self-management features and is never used for unrelated purposes without your consent.

2. Where Your Information Is Stored

  • Supabase (database, authentication, and storage): Stores your profile, records, attached files, and authentication data. Data is separated per user so that only you can access your own data.
  • Local device storage: Stores only non-identifying settings such as language and notification preferences. This data is not transmitted externally.

3. Sharing and Transmission to Third Parties

The App does not sell your information. It transmits information only to the following external services, and only to the extent necessary to provide its features.

The handling of information by these external services is governed by each provider's own privacy policy.

4. Advertising

The App displays banner ads through Google AdMob. Because the App is intended primarily for use by minors, we take the following measures regarding advertising.

The ads in this App are not personalized (non-tracking) ads. We do not send tracking identifiers (such as IDFA) that follow user behavior, and we serve only age-appropriate, general-audience ad content. As a result, the App does not track users across other apps.

5. Minors

The App is intended for use by minors, including middle and high school students. As described above, to protect minors, we disable tracking-based advertising and apply child-directed treatment settings. Date of birth is collected only for age verification and nutrition calculations. We encourage guardians to review this policy regarding their child's use of the App.

6. Location Information

The App uses location information solely to display local weather on the home screen. Location access requires your permission on the device; if you do not grant it, all features other than weather display remain available as usual. Once used to retrieve weather, location data (latitude / longitude) is never stored or accumulated within the App.

7. Camera and Photos

The App may access your photo library to attach photos, videos, or PDFs to training records, and your camera to scan food barcodes. Both are used only in response to your actions.

8. Your Rights

You can view, edit, and delete the information you have entered in the App at any time. If you delete your account, the data associated with it will be deleted. For requests or inquiries regarding data handling, please contact us using the contact information at the end of this policy.

9. Data Protection

The App stores authentication data in a secure area of the device (secure storage) and encrypts communication with servers to help protect your information. However, please note that no method of transmission over the internet can be guaranteed to be completely secure.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may revise this policy as the App's features change or as required by law. If there are significant changes, we will notify you within the App or on this page. The revised policy takes effect when it is posted on this page.

11. Contact

For inquiries regarding this policy, please use the feedback form within the App or the contact information below.
