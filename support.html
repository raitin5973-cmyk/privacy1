Frequently Asked Questions

I want to delete my account

You can delete your account and all related data from "Delete account" on the profile screen in the app. Data cannot be restored after deletion.

What languages are supported?

Japanese, English, Korean, and Chinese are supported. You can switch languages in the app settings.

What devices are supported?

The app supports iPhone (iOS).
